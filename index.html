Who Am I?

Hi I'm Sharvin Rajandran I'm a creative and driven aspiring journalist equipped with the necessary skill set to operate in the digital era of news. I have a keen eye for newsworthy stories, excellent storytelling skills and a nose for the truth. Experience working on a broad subset of news genres, like feature writing, court reporting, sports reporting and opinion pieces have prepared me to function as a multi-faceted journalist in modern society.

I also have experience with practical reporting, researching, writing, editing and producing newsworthy content. As a multi-faceted journalist with proficiency in filming, editing, captioning, scripting and creating media content, I'm able to work with a Content Management System and have an excellent track record when it comes to working with others.

I have excellent time management and organisational skills, and I'm proficient with Adobe Creative Suite softwares, all of which allows me to thrive in a digital newsroom. I'm also able to transition between news mediums, equipped not only with a flair for writing but also the aptitude to produce media content for online and broadcast news.
